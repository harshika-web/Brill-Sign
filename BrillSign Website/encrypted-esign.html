How Encrypted Signing Works in Brillsign

Below is the signing lifecycle:

01

Upload

Document is uploaded to the BrillSign platform.

02

Fingerprint

A unique cryptographic fingerprint of the document is generated.

03

Review

Signer reviews the document and provides mathematical approval.

04

Binding

Signature is mathematically bound to the document content.

05

Sealing

A tamper-evident cryptographic record is permanently created.

06

Verification

The document becomes independently verifiable globally.

Key Outcome

Even if the file is downloaded, emailed, archived, or stored elsewhere — its authenticity can still be proven.
